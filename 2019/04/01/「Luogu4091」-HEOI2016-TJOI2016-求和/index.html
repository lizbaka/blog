problem

Solution

我们知道当i<ji<ji<j时，S(i,j)=0S(i,j)=0S(i,j)=0，所以

∑i=0n∑j=0iS(i,j)×2j×j!\sum_{i=0}^n\sum_{j=0}^iS(i,j)\times 2^j\times j! i=0∑n​j=0∑i​S(i,j)×2j×j!

=∑j=0n2j×j!∑i=0nS(i,j)=\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{i=0}^nS(i,j) =j=0∑n​2j×j!i=0∑n​S(i,j)

将第二类斯特林数的展开式代入得

∑j=0n2j×j!∑i=0n1j!∑k=0j(−1)k×(jk)(j−k)i\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{i=0}^n\frac{1}{j!}\sum_{k=0}^j(-1)^k\times\begin{pmatrix}j\\k\end{pmatrix}(j-k)^i j=0∑n​2j×j!i=0∑n​j!1​k=0∑j​(−1)k×(jk​)(j−k)i

=∑j=0n2j×j!∑i=0n∑k=0j(−1)kk!×(j−k)i(j−k)!=\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{i=0}^n\sum_{k=0}^j\frac{(-1)^k}{k!}\times\frac{(j-k)^i}{(j-k)!} =j=0∑n​2j×j!i=0∑n​k=0∑j​k!(−1)k​×(j−k)!(j−k)i​

=∑j=0n2j×j!∑k=0j(−1)kk!×∑i=0n(j−k)i(j−k)!=\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{k=0}^j\frac{(-1)^k}{k!}\times\frac{\sum_{i=0}^n(j-k)^i}{(j-k)!} =j=0∑n​2j×j!k=0∑j​k!(−1)k​×(j−k)!∑i=0n​(j−k)i​

=∑j=0n2j×j!∑k=0j(−1)kk!×(j−k)n+1−1(j−k−1)(j−k)!=\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{k=0}^j\frac{(-1)^k}{k!}\times\frac{(j-k)^{n+1}-1}{(j-k-1)(j-k)!} =j=0∑n​2j×j!k=0∑j​k!(−1)k​×(j−k−1)(j−k)!(j−k)n+1−1​

后面那玩意看起来很像两个函数的卷积

我们令f(x)=(−1)xx!,g(x)=xn+1−1(x−1)x!f(x)=\frac{(-1)^x}{x!},g(x)=\frac{x^{n+1}-1}{(x-1)x!}f(x)=x!(−1)x​,g(x)=(x−1)x!xn+1−1​

代入得

∑j=0n2j×j!∑k=0jf(k)×g(j−k)\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!\sum_{k=0}^jf(k)\times g(j-k) j=0∑n​2j×j!k=0∑j​f(k)×g(j−k)

=∑j=0n2j×j!(f∗g)(j)=\sum_{j=0}^n2^j\times j!(f*g)(j) =j=0∑n​2j×j!(f∗g)(j)

然后就可以用NTTNTTNTT来预处理出(f∗g)(f*g)(f∗g)了
