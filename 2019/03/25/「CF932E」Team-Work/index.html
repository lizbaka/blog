「CF932E」Team Work

problem

Solution

题意：求∑i=1n(ni)×ik\sum_{i=1}^n\begin{pmatrix}n\\i\end{pmatrix}\times i^k∑i=1n​(ni​)×ik

大力颓柿子，根据常幂转下降幂公式，有

∑i=1n(ni)×ik\sum_{i=1}^n\begin{pmatrix}n\\i\end{pmatrix}\times i^k i=1∑n​(ni​)×ik

=∑i=1n(ni)∑j=0i{kj}×ij‾=\sum_{i=1}^n\begin{pmatrix}n\\i\end{pmatrix}\sum_{j=0}^i\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\times i^{\underline j} =i=1∑n​(ni​)j=0∑i​{kj​}×ij​

=∑i=1n(ni)∑j=0i{kj}×j!(ij)=\sum_{i=1}^n\begin{pmatrix}n\\i\end{pmatrix}\sum_{j=0}^i\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\times j!\begin{pmatrix}i\\j\end{pmatrix} =i=1∑n​(ni​)j=0∑i​{kj​}×j!(ij​)

=∑j=0min(n,k){kj}∑i=jnn!i!(n−i)!×j!×i!j!(i−j)!=\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\sum_{i=j}^n\frac{n!}{i!(n-i)!}\times j! \times \frac{i!}{j!(i-j)!} =j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}i=j∑n​i!(n−i)!n!​×j!×j!(i−j)!i!​

=∑j=0min(n,k){kj}∑i=jnn!(n−i)!×1(i−j)!=\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\sum_{i=j}^n\frac{n!}{(n-i)!}\times \frac{1}{(i-j)!} =j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}i=j∑n​(n−i)!n!​×(i−j)!1​

后面那个求和上下同乘(n−j)!(n-j)!(n−j)!，有

∑j=0min(n,k){kj}∑i=jnn!(n−i)!×1(i−j)×(n−j)!(n−j)!\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\sum_{i=j}^n\frac{n!}{(n-i)!}\times \frac{1}{(i-j)}\times\frac{(n-j)!}{(n-j)!} j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}i=j∑n​(n−i)!n!​×(i−j)1​×(n−j)!(n−j)!​

=∑j=0min(n,k){kj}n!(n−j)!∑i=jn(n−jn−i)=\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\frac{n!}{(n-j)!}\sum_{i=j}^n\begin{pmatrix}n-j\\n-i\end{pmatrix} =j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}(n−j)!n!​i=j∑n​(n−jn−i​)

i<ji<ji<j的时候组合数为000，不妨把iii的下界变为000，于是有

∑j=0min(n,k){kj}n!(n−j)!∑i=0n(n−jn−i)\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\frac{n!}{(n-j)!}\sum_{i=0}^n\begin{pmatrix}n-j\\n-i\end{pmatrix} j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}(n−j)!n!​i=0∑n​(n−jn−i​)

=∑j=0min(n,k){kj}n!(n−j)!×2n−j=\sum_{j=0}^{min(n,k)}\begin{Bmatrix}k\\j\end{Bmatrix}\frac{n!}{(n-j)!}\times 2^{n-j} =j=0∑min(n,k)​{kj​}(n−j)!n!​×2n−j

于是我们可以O(k2)O(k^2)O(k2)递推求出第二类斯特林数，枚举jjj时迭代计算n!(n−j)!\frac{n!}{(n-j)!}(n−j)!n!​，快速幂计算2n−j2^{n-j}2n−j即可
